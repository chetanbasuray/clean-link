CleanLink Privacy Policy

CleanLink is a browser extension that removes tracking and affiliate parameters from URLs you click.

CleanLink does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All cleaning and optional link count statistics are processed locally in your browser using Chrome's storage.local. No browsing history, clicks, or other data is sent to any server.

For more information or to report issues, visit our GitHub repository: https://github.com/username/CleanLink
